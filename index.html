Enroll

Download STEP-app on Playstore/appstore. Register into the STEP Scholarship Exam with an Entry Fee. Enroll yourself now!

Play

Two chapters, one each in Math and Science will be released every 15 days. Participants will be scored and ranked according to their speed, accuracy, and conceptual clarity while playing these chapters within the STEP app.

Score

Play and compete to be the top-ranked on the leader board for your class. Note: Your speed will be evaluated through the average time taken to answer each question. Accuracy and conceptual clarity will be assessed through the percentage of attempted questions answered correctly. Their position will be updated on the STEP Scholarship Exam leaderboard.

Get Verified

After the stipulated time for completing all the scholarship chapters on the STEP app, top scorers will be required to upload documents for verification of identity. Post verification, top scorers on the leaderboard will be invited for a monitored exam at our nearest examination center.

Give monitored exam

A monitored exam will be conducted at various centers across India (colleges, science centers, and other neutral locations). The test will be delivered through the STEP app in its gamified format only.

Qualify

Through the monitored exam, the top 1,100 students from each class will be eligible for scholarships. The top 30 students from each class will further be invited for our televised Grand Finale to compete for the grand prize of Rs. 1 Cr.

Win

The top 1,100 students from each class will receive scholarships from Rs. 12,000 to Rs. 1 Cr, personalized career advice from leading academicians and will also be groomed for future success throughout their academic career.
